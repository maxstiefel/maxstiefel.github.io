ClimateCheck: Heatwaves

ClimateCheck: Heatwaves

Maximilian Stiefel

2022-10-26

Extreme heat return levels across multiple return periods were estimated both empirically and parametrically. We used annual maxima from LOCA data across 32 GCMs for this historical (1950-2005) and RCP 4.5/8.5 projected (2006-2100) periods. The fastest parametric estimation method by far was L-moments. Since this method with the chosen package (R::extRemes::fevd) does not facilitate adjusting for non-stationarity, a MLE method with trend correction is also proposed. 1 day and 5 day heat events were modeled, the latter from 5 day moving window average temperatures.
